Nritanjali School of Dance

Preserving Tradition, Inspiring Creativity

Located in Chathamangalam, Nritanjali is a premier institution dedicated to classical and contemporary performing arts. We provide structured training in Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, Mohiniyattam, Semi-Classical, Western Dance, and Carnatic Music. Our mission is to instill discipline, creativity, and cultural pride in each student.

Our Courses

Explore a wide range of classical and modern art forms suitable for all ages and skill levels.

Bharatanatyam

One of the oldest classical dance forms rooted in devotion and tradition.

Kuchipudi

Known for its elegance, fast rhythms, and expressive storytelling.

Mohiniyattam

A graceful dance style from Kerala emphasizing soft movements and expressions.

Semi-Classical

Combining classical roots with contemporary flair and rhythm.

Western Dance

From Hip-Hop to Bollywood, we teach vibrant modern styles with energy.

Carnatic Music

Vocal training in South Indian classical music with emphasis on technique and devotion.
